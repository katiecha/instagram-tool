# Follower Tracker

## Prerequisite
1. Click the three lines in the top left of your profile
2. Navigate to Account Center
3. Click on "Your information and permissions"
4. Click on "Export your information" --> "Create export" --> "Export to device"
5. Customize information: Select "Followers and following" from the Connections category. Clear out the rest.
6. Date range: Select "All time"
7. Format: Select "JSON"
8. Wait for download to complete
